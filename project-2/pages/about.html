Fake Meat

My parents decided they wanted to become vegetarians before they had my brother or I so as a result I grew up vegetarian. My mom is quite a creative cook which makes vegetarian cooking fun and exciting. As you may have noticed, there are more and more vegetarian meat alternative options in grocery stores these days. You can find 'fake' bacon, sausage, hot dogs, ribs, chicken, and even turkey with a little searching around at your grocery store. These are all great options and usually can be quite high in protein and low in fat which is great. However, sometimes it's fun to create or make your own meat alternatives.

Eggplant is pretty well known for being a vegetable that when prepared in certain ways has a texture and consistency similar to meat. This is especially intriguing for people who previously ate meat and are trying to cut back. Eggplant can give a similar 'satisfaction' that some people crave from meat, all while being much healthier and less imapactful on the evironment. Obviously eggplant doesn't have a lot of protein, but that's where quinoa, rice and beans, nuts, and soy comes in.

This recipe I found is fun, relatively simple, and makes for a delicious dinner. I hope you try it out! Why not?!
